About Me

I was born in Santa Cruz, California. I am the youngest of nine children, of Asian descent with Filipino, Chinese, and Japanese lineage. I currently live in San Leandro, CA, in a house with four cats and three dogs! Not my own pets, but I have grown to love them as my own, which reinforces my love for animals. I do have my own cats, two of them - Leeza and Doobs!

I have a Bachelor's degree in Electronics Engineering, worked for a little over 20 years, mostly, in the tech industry. I worked at Intel for ten years straight out of college and recently, at Lawrence Livermore National Lab for three years. I really wanted to tap into my creative side thus my investment in coding bootcamp to hopefully reignite the passion that I need, to feel inspired in a work place. And at this moment, I am loving every day of it!

I also enjoy performing in the theatre arts, mostly, singing, as a hobby, which definitely taps into my creative side. I love to travel, cook, drink wine, go to beach destinations, watch sunrise and/or sunset. When I am at home, I love cleaning, washing dishes, doing laundry and folding clothes.
